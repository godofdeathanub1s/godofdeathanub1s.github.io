### Neswin is a curious security researcher and aspiring red teamer from the Philippines, began programming at the age of 11. However, due to mental health challenges, he took a break and focused on recovery for several years.

Despite the obstacles, Neswin didn't give up. At 15, he resumed his learning journey. Now 16, he possesses a diverse skill set, ranging from penetration testing and video editing to moderating and graphic design. However, his primary focus lies in practicing red teaming, with a keen interest in binary exploitation and a desire to master malware development. Neswin loves finding 0days too.

Additionally, Neswin is a part of the Tokyo-based hacking team named TenguSec, organized by Cicada, also known as Tripp Roybal.
